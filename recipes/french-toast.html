French Toast

Description

French toast; for mornings when you want to make up to your significant other your behaviour from the previous night. Delicious, full of calories, and a surefire way to beg for forgiveness.

What are you waiting for?

Ingredients

  • 4 egss
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • quarter teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 8 slices bread

Steps

  1. Combine eggs, milk and cinnamon; beat well. Dip bread into egg mixture until completely coated.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Cook bread slices until they are golden brown on both sides. Serve hot.
